About
O'Byrne Construction was founded in 1995 in Los Angeles, CA by Thomas O'Byrne. OBC offers general contracting services and specializes in foundation and framing. With 20 years of experience, O'Byrne Construction has helped many homeowners to create their visions and with homes built to last. Tom has maintained a gleaming reputation of working extremely diligently and always ensuring the highest quality worksmanship for his clients.
Services
Foundation:
Build, erect and align forms for various concrete structures and foundations. Estimate concrete quantities. Instruct and direct laborers and/or personally perform duties of cutting and placing reinforcing wire mesh, preparing appropriate sand and cement mixtures and placing, pouring and rough levelling of concrete. Fine finish concrete by floating, trowelling, edging and brushing.
Framing and Drywall:
Install and fasten panels of drywall into framework. Use spackle to join the drywall panels together in an efficient and quick manner to minimize any imperfections and reinforce the drywall.
General Contracting:
Plan all phases of the construction project. Estimate the costs of materials and staffing; the expense involved in securing permits; and the cost for equipment that must be leased or purchased. Prepare a budget and timetable that establishes milestones for phases of the project to ensure that completion dates are met.
